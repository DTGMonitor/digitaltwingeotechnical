## Naming Rules

Use strategic section language carefully.

For homepage/body content:
- Prefer Capabilities when describing what DTG does.
- Prefer Operating Environments when explaining where DTG works.

For top navigation:
- Use Services as the short navigation label.
- Use Applications as the short navigation label.
- Do not use Markets.
- Do not force long labels like Capabilities or Operating Environments into the top navigation if they reduce usability.

The distinction is:
- Navigation labels should be short and usable.
- Section headings should carry the strategic positioning.
